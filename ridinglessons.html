Riding Lessons

Weekly Lessons For All Abilities

Lessons for the current academic year (2019-2020) cost Â£17 for an hour-long lesson at Urchinwood Manor, which is where the majority of our members ride. This is a significant discount from their usual public lesson price. The size of the lesson groups range from 2 to 5 people, which means that you have the opportunity to learn and develop quickly.

Lessons are allocated weekly on a first come, first served basis and are at additional cost to the annual membership fee. Members simply sign up for lessons when they are available, there is no commitment.

Urchinwood Manor Stables, which we regularly ride at, are approximately a 30 minute drive from Bristol city centre, or a 45 minute bus ride and short walk away via the U1 or U2.

Group Skills
Beginner
  • Lead the horse
  • Run down/up stirrups
  • Tighten/loosen the girth
  • Mount the horse
  • Lengthen/shorten reins
  • Halt-walk transition
  • Change the rein down the centre line
  • Change the rein across the long diagonal
  • 20 metre circle in walk
  • Walk-halt transition
  • Dismount from the horse
Introductory
  • Medium walk
  • Free walk on a long rein
  • Walk without stirrups
  • Change the rein across the short diagonal
  • Walk-trot transition
  • Sitting trot
  • Rising trot
  • Rising on the correct diagonal
  • 20 metre circle in trot
  • Trot-walk transition
  • Turn on the forehand
Preliminary
  • Demonstrate a working trot
  • Trot-canter transition
  • Be able to identify correct/incorrect canter lead
  • Correct an incorrect canter lead (simple change)
  • Trot without stirrups
  • Canter-trot transition
  • 3 loop serpentine in walk
  • Half 10 metre cicle in walk
Novice
  • Change the rein using two half 10 metre circles
  • Canter without stirrups
  • Ask for canter on the correct lead in every transition
  • 3 loop serpentines in trot
  • 20 metre circle in canter
  • Show some lengthened strides in trot
  • Rein back (3-4 strides)
  • Demonstrate a consistent, elastic contact with horse
Elementary
  • Medium trot
  • Medium canter
  • Counter-canter
  • Demonstrate a flying change
  • 4 loop serpentine in trot
  • Shoulder in
  • Leg yield
  • Demonstrate the principles of riding the horse on the bit
Medium-Advanced
  • Extended walk
  • Extended trot
  • 1/2 pirouette in walk
  • Half pass
  • Travers
  • 8 metre circles in trot
  • Half pass in trot
  • Half pass in canter
  • Serpentine in canter with flying changes
